## Interpretation

### Why Pop-weighted Performs Better

1. **Accounts for population distribution:**
   - Weights embeddings by where people actually live
   - More relevant for service delivery indicators
   - Captures urban vs rural dynamics

2. **Education services benefit most:**
   - Schools, teachers, computers naturally follow population
   - Pop-weighted embeddings align with service locations

3. **Infrastructure shows large gains:**
   - Network coverage improves by Δ = +0.205
   - Roads/railways improve by Δ = +0.046
   - These services concentrate where people live

4. **Health and basic utilities improve:**
   - Institutional childbirth +0.114
   - Access to 3 basic services +0.121
   - Services follow population density

### When Regular Might Be Better

Variables showing small preference for regular embeddings:
- Wastewater treatment (environmental, not population-specific)
- Budget execution capacity (institutional, not spatial)
- HIV incidence (transmission patterns different from population density)
