## Design Rationale

### Why User Was Not Satisfied with Previous Palette

**Likely issues with Blue/Green/Orange:**
1. Green (#229954) felt too "environmental" rather than academic
2. Green + Orange combination evoked "traffic signal" association
3. High contrast between all three colors lacked harmony
4. Missing color temperature balance
5. Mid-tone blue was somewhat generic

### Why Navy/Teal/Coral Works Better

**Color harmony:**
- Navy and Teal share blue undertones (cohesion)
- Coral provides warm contrast without clashing
- Balanced color temperature (cool + warm)
- Professional yet modern appearance

**Psychological impact:**
- Navy: Trust, authority, stability (finance, academia)
- Teal: Innovation, clarity, growth (tech, progress)
- Coral: Energy, warmth, approachability (action, human)

**Academic context:**
- Less generic than mid-tone blue
- More sophisticated than lime green
- Suitable for conferences and publications
- Memorable without being flashy
