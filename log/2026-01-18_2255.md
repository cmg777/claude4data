   - Reduces physical capital coefficient bias
   - Improves model fit by 19 percentage points
   - Yields theoretically consistent parameter estimates

2. **Augmented Solow explains ~80% of cross-country income variation**

   - Human capital share: ~1/3

3. **Conditional convergence is real:**
   - No unconditional convergence (except OECD)
   - Strong conditional convergence when controlling for fundamentals
   - Speed: 1.4-2.1% per year
